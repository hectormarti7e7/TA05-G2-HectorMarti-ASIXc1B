EchoMind - Oria

EchoMind - Oria: These are two projects I developed for my second-year final project in the SMX vocational training program. Oria is a voice assistant capable of using operating system functions to make computer use easier for people with physical disabilities. EchoMind is the fictional company that develops Oria.

This voice assistant is designed to assist individuals with physical limitations—such as restricted mobility in hands or arms—which often hinders their autonomy and digital accessibility
